# Product Requirements Document: Community Portfolio Page

## Overview

A single-page portfolio website showcasing Lidia's Developer Advocay work, community work, speaking engagements, technical and non-technical workshops positioning Lidia as a Technical Community Builder Developer Advocate

## Goals

1. Provide quick, visual access to Lidia's best community management work
2. Make it easy for hiring managers to understand the scope and impact of her work
3. Create a professional, memorable impression that aligns with creator-focused roles
4. Complement LinkedIn profile with concrete examples of work

## Target Audience

- Hiring managers and recruiters at tech companies
- Specifically: Ben at Lenovo for Global Creator Community Manager role
- Decision makers looking for evidence of community building and creator collaboration skills

## Core Requirements

### Content Sections

**1. Header/Hero Section**
- Professional headshot or profile image
- Name and professional title
- Short tagline about community building expertise
- Social links: LinkedIn, GitHub, Instagram, X (Twitter)

**2. About Me Section**
- Brief introduction to Lidia's background
- Key areas of expertise
- Current focus and passion

**3. Featured Work Examples**
Must showcase these three specific projects:
- Co-Facilitator: AI for Business Workshop (Entrepreneurs & Builders)
- Talk Lead: Community-Centered Tech & Advocacy
- Panelist: Google DevFest (Women Techmakers / GDG)

**4. Each Work Example Must Include**
- Project title and role
- Organization/context
- Brief description of the work
- Lidia's specific contributions and approach
- Link to LinkedIn post for verification
- Tags for quick categorization (e.g., "Workshop Facilitation", "Public Speaking", "Panel Discussion")

### Design Requirements

**Visual Style**
- Clean, minimalist design inspired by nnennahacks.com
- Professional but approachable aesthetic
- Strong typography hierarchy
- Ample white space
- Mobile responsive

**Color Scheme**
- Primary Black: #000000
- Navy Blue: #14213d
- Orange Accent: #fca311
- Light Gray: #e5e5e5
- White: #ffffff

**Brand Alignment**
- Should feel modern and tech-forward
- Reflects community-first values
- Accessible and inclusive design choices

### User Experience

**Primary User Flow**
1. Land on page
2. Quickly understand who Lidia is
3. Browse featured work examples
4. Click through to LinkedIn for full background
5. Easy way to contact or connect

**Key Interactions**
- Smooth scrolling
- Hover states on work items
- Click to expand details (optional)
- One-click access to LinkedIn

### Success Metrics

**Immediate**
- Page loads in under 2 seconds
- All work examples clearly visible
- Mobile friendly (50%+ of viewers may use mobile)

**Short-term**
- Positive feedback from Ben at Lenovo
- Can be easily shared via link
- Works across all major browsers

### Technical Constraints

- Single HTML file (for easy sharing and hosting)
- No backend required
- Can be hosted on free platforms (GitHub Pages, Netlify, Vercel)
- Works without JavaScript (progressive enhancement)

### Content Guidelines

**Tone**
- Professional but personable
- Results-focused without being boastful
- Clear and concise descriptions

**Length**
- Project descriptions: 2-3 sentences each
- Role descriptions: 1-2 sentences
- Total page: scannable in under 2 minutes
